Frequently Asked Questions

🎯 How do I use Ideas?

Ideas is a creative jar-based idea management app where you:

  • Create Jars: Organize your ideas by theme (work projects, creative writing, business ideas, etc.)
  • Add Ideas: Capture inspiration with custom fonts and beautiful backgrounds
  • Shake to Discover: Shake your device to randomly pick an idea from any jar
  • Customize: Choose from beautiful fonts and background images to make each jar unique

📱 How do I shake to get a random idea?

Simply open any jar that contains ideas and give your phone a gentle shake! The app will randomly select one of your ideas. This feature only works when there are ideas in the jar.

🎨 How can I customize my jars?

When creating or editing a jar, you can:

  • Choose from beautiful custom fonts
  • Select background images that match your theme
  • Edit jar titles and themes anytime

🌍 Is the app available in other languages?

Yes! Ideas supports English, Spanish (Español), and French (Français). The app automatically uses your device's language setting.

🔒 Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely! All your ideas are stored locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or ideas to external servers.

🐛 How can I report a bug?

If you encounter any issues, please send us an email with:

  • A detailed description of the problem
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots if applicable

We'll investigate and fix it promptly!

💡 How can I request a new feature?

We love hearing from our creative users! Send us your feature suggestions via email. Popular requests include:

  • New customization options
  • Additional jar organization features
  • Export and sharing capabilities

🛠 Technical Support

If you're experiencing technical difficulties, please include the following information in your email to help us assist you better:

  • Device model: (iPhone 15 Pro, iPhone 14, etc.)
  • iOS version: (Go to Settings > General > About)
  • Ideas app version: (Check in the App Store)
  • Description of the issue: What exactly is happening?
  • Steps to reproduce: What were you doing when the problem occurred?
  • Screenshots: If applicable, visual examples help us understand the issue
